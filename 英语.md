```
class Dropout(_DropoutNd):
    r"""During training, randomly zeroes some of the elements of the input tensor with probability :attr:`p`.

    The zeroed elements are chosen independently for each forward call and are sampled from a Bernoulli distribution.

    Each channel will be zeroed out independently on every forward call.

    This has proven to be an effective technique for regularization and
    preventing the co-adaptation of neurons as described in the paper
    `Improving neural networks by preventing co-adaptation of feature
    detectors`_ .
    Furthermore, the outputs are scaled by a factor of :math:`\frac{1}{1-p}` during training. This means that during evaluation the module simply computes an
identity function.
```

翻译：（头重脚轻）

训练阶段，输入tensors的一些元素将会以 p 的概率置零

每一轮前向传播，从伯努利分布独立采样置零元素

每一轮前向传播，每个通道都会独立地被置零

就像论文“通过避免特征检测器相互适应（过拟合）来改进神经网络”，因为正则化和阻止了神经元的co-adaptation，这项技术已经被证明有效

训练阶段，$\frac{1}{1-p}$ 用来放缩输出outputs。这意味着这个模块在验证阶段只简单计算恒等映射。
